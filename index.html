Mission Statement

We, the National Association, hold to our beliefs. Our members are indeed bonafide subscribers to our mission. We see the world as a place and its people as its inhabitants. We believe there is a difference between truth and falsehood and that every human being is a part of humanity. Specifically that suffrage means the right to vote, crime is illegal, and that rights belong to the right holders.
